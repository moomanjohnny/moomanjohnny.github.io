A brand new story.

You play as Anna, a test subject, who slept in for 18 years. She woke up to find that Aperture isn't what it used to be, and along the way she found a cheerful, excited, and optimistic core, Alex. Little do they know that what's ahead is not just the everyday testing track.

New test elements.

Timex will include new testing elements, such as a time slowing button.

Over 65 new maps.

Timex will have over 65 new maps; 40 of those are Singleplayer, 15 are Co-op, and the rest are bonus puzzles included for your enjoyment.

Best of all: It's free.

Timex is completely free, as long as you have already purchased Portal 2 on Steam.
